SAR Call-outs in the
North Shore Mountains
[2018 - 2020]

I compiled location data for search and rescue call-outs by going through Emergency Management BC's reports and Facebook posts. Using that data, I created a Leaflet heatmap of all of the call-outs in the North Shore Mountains.

UBC SEEDS
Compost Bin
Contamination

My team was tasked to create an inventory of compost bins on campus and to collect accessibility and contamination data. We wanted to know if there was any correlation between bin accessibility and the rate of contamination.
