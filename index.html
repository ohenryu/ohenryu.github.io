About Me

Henry graduated from UBC with a major in Human Geography in 2020, then continued to further his education at BCIT, earning an Advanced Diploma in GIS. His passion lies in analyzing demographic data, particularly in the fields of urban planning and location intelligence. As a lover of maps and data, Henry aims to improve the world through geospatial analysis and help people understand"the scienc of where". Woof woof! I am a shiba!
